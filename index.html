Your Mental Health is Our Priority! Purpose

Social media has become an integral part of our society the number of users is only on the rise. Recent research has indicated that social media harms one’s mental health, with millions of people being affected worldwide. This study aims to understand if a social media platform can be created that is beneficial to one’s mental health rather than damaging. To do so, research has been conducted on the specific features which make social media harmful, and they have been excluded. Furthermore, measures associated with increased mental well-being have been included in the project.
